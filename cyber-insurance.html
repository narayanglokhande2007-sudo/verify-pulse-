Frequently Asked Questions

How long does integration take? +
Integration typically takes 2-4 weeks depending on your existing infrastructure. Our technical team provides full support throughout the process.
Can we white-label the solution? +
Yes, VerifyPulse is fully white-labelable. Your brand will appear throughout the customer experience, and you can customize the user interface to match your branding.
What data do you collect from our customers? +
We follow a strict zero-PII policy. We analyze URLs and email content for threats but never store personal information. All data is processed in ephemeral memory and discarded immediately after analysis.
How do you ensure data privacy and compliance? +
VerifyPulse is fully compliant with GDPR, CCPA, and Indian data protection regulations. We undergo regular security audits and maintain ISO 27001 certification.
What kind of support do you provide? +
We provide 24/7 technical support, dedicated account management, quarterly business reviews, and regular training for your team.
Can we customize the threat detection rules? +
Yes, our platform is highly customizable. You can adjust threat detection sensitivity, create custom threat lists, and define specific rules for your customer base.
